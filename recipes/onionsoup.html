Onion Soup

Description

Delicious onion soup made with onion and onion and onion and onion

Other ingredients include stock, herbs, butter, and onion.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Beef Stock
  • Butter
  • Cognac
  • Bay Leaf
  • Thyme and Rosemary
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the onions in the butter until very soft, at least 1 hour
  2. Add stock and bring to boil, reduce heat to simmer
  3. Add cognac and herbs, simmer for 20 minutes
  4. Serve with parmesan cheese over crouton
