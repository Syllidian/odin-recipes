Tteokbokki

Description

Very spicy very delicious Korean Rice Cake

Gochujang very spicy owie.

Ingredients

  • Rice Cakes
  • Gochujang
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Scallion
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Soak rice cakes while preparing other ingredients
  2. Melt butter in pan, add garlic and cook 30 seconds
  3. Add gochujang, soy sauce, and sesame oil, cook until oil breaks out
  4. Add rice cakes, with whatever water clings to them
  5. Cook until caramelized, add scallions and sesame seeds
